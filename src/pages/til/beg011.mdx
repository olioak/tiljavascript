You can show numbers instead of strings. Like this `console.log(1)`.

Because the **1** is not surrounded by quotes, it's not considered a string.

You can also add numbers in JavaScript. `1+1` is going to show **2**.

```javascript
console.log(1);
console.log(1 + 1);
```
